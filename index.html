About Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV)

Established in 1964

Initially known as the University of Indore, later renamed in honor of Queen Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar.

NAAC Accredited 'A+'

Consistently ranks among the top universities in India in various national rankings like NIRF.

Diverse Programs

Offers undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs across multiple disciplines.

200+ Affiliated Colleges

Wide network of affiliated colleges offering diverse courses in various streams.

Academic Excellence

DAVV offers programs in science, arts, commerce, law, management, engineering, education, and social sciences through numerous academic departments and affiliated colleges.

Modern Facilities

The university provides modern amenities like libraries, sports complexes, hostels, and research labs to support student life and academic pursuits.

Vibrant Community

DAVV boasts a vibrant student community with cultural events, fests, and extracurricular activities happening throughout the academic year.

Global Collaborations

The university has collaborations with national and international institutions, facilitating academic exchange programs, research partnerships, and joint projects.

About International Institute of Professional Studies (IIPS)

Pioneer in Professional Education

Established in 1992, IIPS is a pioneer institute providing a new dimension to professional education under Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), Indore.

Academic Excellence

IIPS is ranked among the top institutes in central India for professional studies, recognized in both national and international academic circles.

Diverse Programs

Offers integrated and specialized courses in management, computer science, commerce, advertisement, tourism, and public relations management.

Expert Faculty

Boasts highly qualified faculty, most holding doctorates and actively involved in research and consultancy.

Infrastructure and Facilities

IIPS boasts modern infrastructure with well-equipped classrooms, computer labs, a library, seminar halls, and internet access to support the educational needs of students.

Industry Connections

Strong ties with industry, regular internships, guest lectures, and industrial visits provide students with real-world exposure.

Placement Opportunities

Active placement cell ensuring ample opportunities with top companies in India and abroad across various sectors.

Student Life

Vibrant student culture with various clubs and societies promoting extracurricular activities such as debates, cultural fests, and sports events.

Conference Objectives

Interdisciplinary Dialogue

Facilitate interdisciplinary dialogue on SDG implementation strategies.

Showcase Success

Showcase successful multidisciplinary approaches to SDG challenges.

Cross-sector Collaboration

Identify opportunities for cross-sector collaboration and innovation.

Explore Solutions

Explore viable solutions and share best practices for sustainable societies.

Proposed Themes

Integration of Sustainability Across Dimensions

Exploring holistic approaches to sustainability in various sectors and disciplines.

Technological Innovation for Sustainable Development

Leveraging cutting-edge technologies to address sustainability challenges.

Empowering Change through Education and Training for SDGs

Developing educational strategies to promote and achieve Sustainable Development Goals.
